WHO ARE WE?
Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology
SLIIT(Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology) is a leading non-state university approved by University Grants Commission(UGC) and also a member of Association of Commonwealth Universities(ACU), as well as the International Association of Universities(IAU). SLIIT is the first Sri Lankan institute to be accredited by the Institution of Engineering & Technology, UK. SLIIT was established in 1999 with 400 students in Colombo metro campus. Currently, we have over 9000 students, local and international.

OUR MISSION
To create a learning and research environment with best possible resources for our students and staff to be innovative and dedicated to excellence and to produce graduated with strong analytical, problem solving and communication skills.

OUR OBJECTIVE
Education & training of professionals at a high level of excellence in the fields of IT, buisness and engineering assisting and promoting enteprises, innovators and start-up companies with the necessary expertise to achieve business objectives provisions of consultancy services and software services to public and private sector enterprises of Sri Lanka at a reasonable cost conduct and promotion of research & development leading ot specialized applications, services and innovative technology products, Expanding horizons in the fields of IT, buisness and engineering to reach international level.
